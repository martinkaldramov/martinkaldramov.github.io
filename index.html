About Me

Hello everyone, this is a brief introduction to my technology background, what drives me and other passions.

I believe that the experience and the skills that I've accumulated over the years, supporting different IT products and environments, make me a very well rounded support engineer, ready to face any challenge.

When faced by a problem, i really like to examine it from every possible angle and consider different situations, rather than being satisfied with the first or the easiest answer that comes to mind (or some workaround for it). I believe that there is a huge benefit to this approach - this way almost every time something new and useful can be learned, which then can be applied to other problems and situations.

I developed passion for technology in my early ages (seeing my friend's older brother input commands in the Linux terminal was like seeing magic). Aside for it being my passion, i believe that understanding it and the ability to use it is essential nowadays more than ever (predictions are that in 2020 30 billion objects will have online capabilities, compared to 8.4 billion in 2017).
